РЮ» How does sleep work? Why do we need to sleep?                                 

РЈ║ Brain cycles through 4-5 sleep stages (~90 min each):

  - NREM 1-2: Light sleep, memory consolidation begins
  - NREM 3 (deep/slow-wave): Cell repair, immune function, growth hormone
  release
  - REM: Dream state, emotional processing, memory integration

  Why needed:

  - Memory: Hippocampus replays experiences Рєњ transfers to long-term cortex
  storage
  - Waste clearance: Glymphatic system flushes neurotoxins (amyloid-beta, tau) Рђћ
   only works during sleep
  - Metabolic repair: Protein synthesis, tissue repair, glucose regulation reset
  - Immune: Cytokine production peaks during sleep
  - Synaptic homeostasis: Brain prunes weak synapses, strengthens important ones

  Skip sleep Рєњ cascade failure: cognition drops, immune weakens, cardiovascular
  risk rises, metabolic dysregulation. Chronic deprivation linked to Alzheimer's
   (toxin buildup).

  No species found yet that doesn't sleep. Evolutionary pressure to remain
  unconscious and vulnerable = benefit must be enormous.

РЮ» How does human memory work? Why do we forget things?                          

РЈ║ Memory formation: 3 stages

  1. Encoding Рђћ sensory input Рєњ electrical pattern in neurons. Attention gates
  this: no attention = no encoding
  2. Consolidation Рђћ pattern stabilizes via protein synthesis. Sleep critical
  here (hippocampus replays Рєњ cortex)
  3. Retrieval Рђћ reactivate pattern. Each retrieval slightly rewrites memory
  (reconsolidation)

  Storage types:

  - Working memory: ~4 items, seconds-long, prefrontal cortex
  - Episodic: personal events, hippocampus-dependent
  - Semantic: facts/concepts, distributed cortex
  - Procedural: skills, basal ganglia + cerebellum Рђћ most durable

  Why forget:

  - Decay: unused neural connections weaken (synaptic pruning)
  - Interference: similar memories compete Рђћ new overwrites old (retroactive),
  old blocks new (proactive)
  - Retrieval failure: memory exists but no cue to access it (tip-of-tongue
  phenomenon)
  - Motivated forgetting: emotional trauma Рєњ active suppression
  - Encoding failure: never stored to begin with (most "forgetting" is this)

  Key insight: forgetting isn't bug Рђћ it's feature. Brain discards low-signal
  data to reduce noise, improve pattern recognition, prevent interference.
  Borges' "Funes the Memorious" Рђћ perfect memory = cognitive paralysis.

  Hippocampus damage (Alzheimer's, H.M. case) Рєњ can't form new episodic memories
   but old procedural skills intact. Different systems, different
  vulnerabilities.

РЮ» Why is the sky blue during the day and orange/red at sunset?                  

РЈ║ Rayleigh scattering.

  Sunlight = all wavelengths mixed. Atmosphere full of NРѓѓ/OРѓѓ molecules much
  smaller than light wavelengths.

  Scattering intensity РѕЮ 1/╬╗РЂ┤ Рђћ shorter wavelength scatters far more.

  - Blue (~450nm) scatters ~5.5├Ќ more than red (~700nm)
  - Blue bounces in all directions across sky Рєњ eye sees blue from everywhere
  overhead
  - Red/green scatter less Рєњ pass through mostly straight

  Why sunset orange/red:

  Light path through atmosphere at horizon = much longer (tangential slice vs
  overhead). Blue fully scattered away before reaching eye. Only long-wavelength
   red/orange survives full journey.

  Clean air Рєњ yellow-orange sunset. Dust/smoke/humidity particles scatter
  mid-wavelengths too Рєњ deeper red, sometimes purple.

  Why not violet? Violet scatters even more than blue. Two reasons sky isn't
  violet:
  1. Sun emits less violet to begin with
  2. Human eye less sensitive to violet
  3. Some violet absorbed upper atmosphere

  Net result: brain perceives blue.

РЮ» How does gravity work? Why do objects fall?                                   

РЈ║ Two answers: Newton (practical) vs Einstein (actual).

  ---
  Newton: force at distance

  Every mass attracts every other mass:

  F = G ├Ќ (mРѓЂ ├Ќ mРѓѓ) / r┬▓

  - Works perfectly for engineering, orbits, everyday physics
  - Explains how much but not why
  - Treated gravity as mysterious action-at-a-distance

  ---
  Einstein: curved spacetime (GR, 1915)

  Mass doesn't pull Рђћ mass warps spacetime geometry. Objects follow straightest
  possible path (geodesic) through curved spacetime. That path curves toward
  mass.

  Analogy: heavy ball on rubber sheet creates depression. Nearby ball rolls
  toward it Рђћ not because "attracted," but because geometry of sheet curves that
   way.

  Objects don't fall because pulled. They fall because straight-line path 
  through curved spacetime points down.

  Light bends near stars Рђћ confirmed 1919. No mass, still follows curved
  geometry.

  ---
  Why Earth stays in orbit (not falls into Sun):
  - Moving sideways fast enough that curved path = closed loop
  - "Falling around" the Sun, never hitting

  ---
  What we still don't know:

  - GR and quantum mechanics incompatible Рђћ gravity has no quantum theory yet
  - Graviton (hypothetical carrier particle) never detected
  - Dark matter: extra gravitational effect with no visible mass source
  - Dark energy: gravity somehow losing to expansion at cosmic scale

  GR confirmed to extraordinary precision Рђћ GPS satellites require GR
  corrections or drift 10km/day. But at quantum scale, theory breaks. Still open
   problem.
